## Full description

FCA Contractor Command puts your entire contracting business in one mobile workspace — built for teams competing at PlanHub, Procore, and enterprise scale.

**Lead pipeline** — Capture opportunities, qualify bids, and move work from first contact to signed contract.

**Job sites** — Track active projects, field milestones, and next actions from every job.

**Plan room** — Access drawings, specs, and submittals shared with your team.

**Billing** — View invoices and payment status from your customer portal.

**Communications** — Send updates to your FCA success team and keep message history in one place.

**Customer success** — Open support cases and track resolution without leaving the app.

**Workforce training** — Assign Academy programs, safety modules, and trade credentials your crews need on site.

Every screen connects to the same FCA platform you use on the web — one account, one operating picture.
